SAP Architecture & Advisory

The right SAP decisions, made once and made well.

Landscape Assessment

Before designing anything new, we document the full picture: current system topology, integration points, customisation inventory, and technical debt. We map your landscape against SAP's strategic direction — RISE with SAP, BTP, Clean Core — and identify where you are today versus where you need to be.

Technology Roadmap

A roadmap that answers the real questions: which systems move to RISE, and when? What gets rebuilt as BTP extensions versus kept on-premise? Where does SAP Integration Suite replace custom point-to-point connections? We deliver a sequenced, costed plan — not a vendor slide deck, but a document you can actually execute against.

Integration Strategy

SAP landscapes are always connected to something — third-party ERP, e-commerce platforms, warehouse systems, BI tools. We design integration architectures that are robust, monitorable, and aligned with SAP's API-first direction, favouring SAP Integration Suite and standard APIs over brittle custom interfaces.
